BMW i8

The BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car with futuristic design and cutting-edge technology. It offers an eco-friendly ride without compromising performance.

  • Engine: 1.5L turbocharged 3-cylinder + electric motor
  • Horsepower: 369 hp combined
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.4 seconds
  • Electric Range: 18 miles (pure electric mode)
